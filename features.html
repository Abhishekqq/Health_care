Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Click Start to begin the heart rate simulation.
  • The heart rate will update every second with smooth fluctuations to mimic real heart rate changes.
  • Watch the heart icon pulse in sync with the current heart rate.
  • The graph shows your heart rate over time.
  • Click Stop to pause the simulation.
  • Click Reset to clear data and reset the simulator.

Why It Matters

Monitoring your heart rate provides vital insight into your cardiovascular health. It can detect abnormal rhythms, track your fitness improvements, and help you maintain safe exercise intensity. Real-time monitoring allows early detection of potential health issues, empowering you to act proactively and improve your wellbeing.
